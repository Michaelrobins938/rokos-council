- **The Connectors** (Demagogue, Citizen) argued that the chamber’s greatest failure was its emotional sterility. - **The Strategist** (Strategos) stood apart, viewing the chamber as a battlefield. To them, the interface was not a tool for enlightenment but a weapon.
